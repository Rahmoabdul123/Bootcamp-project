Bootcamp 2021: Fun with circles

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). I created bouncing balls where they are all in different sizes and different shades. If certain keys are pressed and held on (E,R,T) different colours are produced. If 1 is pressed the sizes will increase whilst pressing 0 will decrease the sizes.
